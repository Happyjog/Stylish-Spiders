Programs

Topper Tots is a nurturing and enriching daycare program designed for young children, focusing on early childhood development in a safe, stimulating environment. We offer a balanced curriculum that encourages creativity, socialization, and academic growth through play, structured activities, and outdoor exploration. Our experienced, caring staff are dedicated to providing individualized attention to each child, fostering their emotional, cognitive, and physical well-being. With age-appropriate learning tools, interactive lessons, and a warm, supportive atmosphere, Topper Tots ensures that your child feels confident, happy, and ready for their next steps in their educational journey.


Age Ranges

At Topper Tots, we proudly serve children from six weeks to kindergarten, offering age-appropriate care and educational experiences tailored to each developmental stage. Infants (6 weeks to 12 months) receive nurturing care, with activities that promote sensory development and bonding. Toddlers (12 months to 3 years) engage in interactive play, fostering language, motor skills, and socialization. Preschoolers (3 to 5 years) enjoy a structured curriculum that encourages creativity, early literacy, and problem-solving. For children preparing for kindergarten, our program focuses on building essential skills in readiness for school, including social, emotional, and academic development in a fun, engaging environment.
